ABOUT ME

Just Take A Few Minutes To Learn Some Details About Me


Hello! My name is Devon Jacob. I am a wedding photographer and I’d like to take a second to tell you why I am drawn to photography. Trust and Creativity are two ways in which I will show you the magical world of stopping time. I’m constantly inspired by the beauty and honesty found in all kinds of passionate human beings, and believe that the greatest adventure two people can share in this life is the path you take together in any form of intimacy and connection.

I’m grateful you’ve come this far. Take your time and explore my gallery, you will be amazed at my work. I revel in creating happy, natural photos of wonderful people who just happen to be madly in love.
